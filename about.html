About Gusteau's

Located in the heart of Paris's beautiful and vibrant historic downtown, come indulge yourself with our award-winning menu and dedicated service in a unique French-Bistro atmosphere. Gusteaus in Paris has all the old-world charm of any romantic hideaway in Paris. In line with the French traditional way of cooking, every dish is made from carefully selected high quality fresh products and ingredients, prepared in-house and cooked to perfection with a modern twist. Executive Chef Remy and Owner Linguini welcome you!

About Chef Remy

Remy Ratatouille is an internationally recognized French chef, television personality, and author working in the United States. Since the late 1990s, he has appeared on French and American television and written an array of cookbooks that have become best sellers. Chef Remy began his notable career in 1980 as an apprentice with the Catering College in Aachen, Germany. He continued his training in Switzerland, where he worked in Zurich, Lausanne and Geneva, and also in his home of France. Chef Remy has participated successfully in several chef competitions, twice being awarded a gold medal and challenge trophy at the Hotel Olympia, he has spear headed three pretigious James Beard award wins for Gusteau's. Recently, he was featured on the Food Network's "The Best Of" segment.
