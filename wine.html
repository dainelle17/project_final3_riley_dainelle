The Gusteau Winery produces around 600,000 gallons of wine each year, handcrafted from only the finest grapes and fruit available. Trucks packed with up to 20 tons of wine grapes from all over Europe and grape-growing regions around the country are transported straight from the vineyard to our winery to begin the delicate winemaking process. We understand that the best fruit produces the best wine and our relationships with top growers throughout the country allows us to source from regions only where growing conditions have been ideal. We are selective and buying the very best fruits from areas where seasonal conditions have resulted in the year’s most outstanding varietals, giving us the ability to consistently produce-award winning wines. Since opening in late 2007, Gusteau’s has received over 300 wine awards from various local, national, and international wine competitions.
